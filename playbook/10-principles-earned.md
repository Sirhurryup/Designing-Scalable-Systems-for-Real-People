# Principles Earned

> "Experience teaches lessons. Reflection transforms lessons into principles."

This document serves as the inbox for new principles discovered during consulting engagements.

Every engagement should conclude by asking one question:

> **What principle did I earn today?**

Only principles that prove timeless through practice should be promoted into the permanent chapters of the SirhurryUp Consulting Playbook.

---

# Engagement 09
## Pinnacle Financial Group
### Highly Available Web Platform

---

## Mindset

### Design Before You Deploy

Great engineers never begin in the AWS Console. They begin by understanding the business problem, defining success, and designing the architecture before creating the first resource.

---

### Verify Before You Scale

One correctly functioning server is more valuable than three broken ones. Validate the architecture before introducing automation and elasticity.

---

## Workflow

### Why → Dependencies → Build → Verify → Document → Reflect

Understanding why a resource exists and what it depends upon creates stronger engineers than memorizing implementation steps.

---

### Budget Before You Build

Cost management is part of engineering. Responsible cloud engineers estimate and monitor cost before deploying infrastructure.

---

## Communication

### Explain Business Value Before Technology

Clients purchase business outcomes, not AWS services. Every recommendation should begin with business need before discussing implementation.

---

### Architecture Is A Conversation

Strong architectures emerge through thoughtful questions, active listening, and clear explanations among technical and business stakeholders.

---

## Architecture

### Business Requirements Drive Technology

Business objectives define the destination. AWS services become the implementation chosen to reach those objectives.

---

### Build The Foundation First

Stable cloud environments begin with networking. Every additional service depends upon a properly designed foundation.

---

### Dependencies Matter

Every AWS resource exists because another resource depends upon it. Understanding relationships between services is more valuable than memorizing individual services.

---

### Document Decisions, Not Services

Great documentation explains why architectural decisions were made instead of simply listing AWS resources.

---

### Design For Tomorrow

Good architectures solve today's problems while preparing for tomorrow's growth.

---

## Security

### Trust Identities, Not Networks

Security Groups establish trust relationships. Resources should trust known identities rather than broad network ranges whenever possible.

---

### Minimize The Attack Surface

Expose only the services required for business operations. Everything else should remain inaccessible by default.

---

### Network Design Is The First Layer Of Security

Security begins with thoughtful architecture before any firewall rule is written.

---

## Documentation

### Build Once. Document Forever.

Cloud infrastructure is temporary. Engineering knowledge, documentation, and evidence become permanent organizational assets.

---

### Verification Completes The Work

Infrastructure is not complete when it has been deployed. It is complete when it has been verified from the customer's perspective.

---

## Career

### Learn From The Lesson. Never Copy The Lesson.

Training teaches technology. Consulting applies technology to solve original business problems. Extract principles. Build original engagements.
