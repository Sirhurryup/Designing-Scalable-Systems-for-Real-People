# Engagement 09 — Pinnacle Financial Group

> Designing a Highly Available Web Platform with Elastic Load Balancing and Auto Scaling

---

# Executive Summary

Pinnacle Financial Group engaged SirhurryUp Corporation to design, deploy, and validate a highly available web platform capable of automatically responding to changing customer demand while reducing operational complexity.

Rather than approaching the engagement as an AWS implementation exercise, every architectural decision was driven by business objectives, validated through evidence, and documented using a repeatable consulting methodology.

The completed solution demonstrated standardized infrastructure provisioning, resilient application delivery, automated scaling, and disciplined engineering validation.

---

# Business Problem

The client required a cloud platform capable of supporting business growth without increasing manual operational effort.

Primary objectives included:

- Improve application availability.
- Standardize server deployments.
- Reduce operational complexity.
- Automatically respond to changing customer demand.
- Maintain responsible cloud cost management.
